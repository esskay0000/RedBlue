By Elise Labott, CNN Global Affairs Correspondent 



Updated 4:34 PM ET, Mon April 11, 2016 



Hiroshima, Japan (CNN)U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry on Monday assailed GOP presidential candidate Donald Trump -- though not by name -- for raising the prospect that Japan and South Korea could seek their own nuclear weapons during a visit to Hiroshima, Japan, a city that knows the horrors of nuclear warfare firsthand.







CNN's Holly Yan and Deborah Bloom contributed to this report.
