By John McNamara, Director of Markets for Pivit



Updated 8:49 AM ET, Thu March 24, 2016 

 (CNN)With the bulk of the primary contests completed for the 2016 Presidential election, we still see continued disparity between the state of the Democratic and Republican party nominations. The two parties have had markedly different market movement since last summer; today, the Democratic nomination for Hillary Clinton is largely secured, but the same cannot be said for Donald Trump.
