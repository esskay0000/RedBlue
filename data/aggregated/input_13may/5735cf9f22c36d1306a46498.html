Reid: McConnell must agree with Trump that women are 'dogs and pigs'

Eliza Collins, USA TODAY 2:13 p.m. EDT May 12, 2016
194 Shares

Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell, R-Ky., and Senate Minority Leader Harry Reid, D-Nev., stand together on Capitol Hill, February 24, 2016 in Washington, DC. (Photo: Drew Angerer, Getty Images)

Harry Reid is not mincing words.

"Since Sen. McConnell has so enthusiastically embraced Trump, we can only assume he agrees with Trump's view that women are dogs and pigs,” Reid said about his counterpart, Majority Leader Mitch McConnell, on the Senate floor Thursday, according to Roll Call.

The Senate Democratic leader was speaking ahead of a series of meetings between Trump, McConnell and other Republican leaders. McConnell has said he will support Trump.

"When they get together, they can talk about their policies about being anti-woman,” Reid said.

Reid's bashing of McConnell comes just one day after a tense exchange between Reid and Florida Democratic Rep. Alan Grayson, according to Politico. After Grayson taunted Reid to “say my name,” Reid snapped back: “I want you to lose."
