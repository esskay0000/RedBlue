 (CNN)Donald Trump said Wednesday that he plans to release his taxes when an IRS audit is completed, despite telling the Associated Press the previous day that he would not release his tax documents.

In interview I told @AP that my taxes are under routine audit and I would release my tax returns when audit is complete, not after election!



CNN's Tal Kopan and Julia Manchester contributed to this report.
