Passenger: TSA Laughed at Mile-Long Lines

TSA agents stood around and cracked jokes while passengers waited over four hours

Kit Daniels - May 16, 2016 Comments

TSA agents laughed at the long lines of passengers waiting over four hours to pass security, according to a passenger.

Infowars producer Alejandra G., who boarded a flight at Chicago O’Hare International Airport over the weekend, revealed how the TSA mocked the thousands of passengers missing their flights while standing in mile-long lines.

“They were being very non-chalant and they found humor in the situation,” she said. “They were leaning up against their stations and joked when I came up and asked them about the long lines.”

“They tried to make it seem like it wasn’t a big deal.”
