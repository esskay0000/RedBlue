By Ted Barrett and Manu Raju, CNN



Updated 6:48 PM ET, Tue May 10, 2016 

Washington (CNN)Top Senate Republicans closed the door Tuesday to the possibility of confirming Supreme Court nominee Merrick Garland in a post-election lame duck session -- even if Hillary Clinton wins the White House -- reiterating their long-held position that a new president should pick someone to fill the vacancy on the high court.
