With the Republican nomination all but wrapped up, prominent figures of the anti-Trump movement are getting desperate — really desperate.

Katie Packer, who is the founder of the anti-Trump super PAC Our Principles PAC and a former top aide to Mitt Romney, jokingly tweeted out a call for Jim Gilmore.

Gilmore, a former Virginia governor, who briefly ran for president but failed to gain any traction, dropped out in February, but even that failed to make enough of a splash.

So....is Jim Gilmore still an active candidate?  Asking for a friend....

Trump is now the presumed nominee for the Republican Party after Texas Sen. Ted Cruz dropped out of the race Tuesday night and Ohio Gov. John Kasich is expected to drop out Wednesday evening. Reince Priebus, chairman of the Republican National Committee, has called on the party to unite behind Trump.
