(CNN)Four more years for Vice President Joe Biden?

That was the joke Wednesday morning when he was busy meeting with Central American leaders.
    Asked about Donald Trump's elevation to presumptive Republican nominee, Biden quipped, "I anticipate he'll ask me to be vice president."
    After some laughs, he followed-up, "I have nothing serious to say."
    After spending months weighing his own presidential run, Biden announced in October he would forgo a run. He will, however, campaign heavily for the eventual Democratic nominee, according to aides.
